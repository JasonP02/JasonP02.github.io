## Overview
Two-semester research project focused on autonomous manipulation of water-filled cups using the LoCoBot platform.

## Technical Details
• Implemented ROS control systems
• Developed computer vision pipeline
• Created trajectory planning algorithms
• Built liquid dynamics compensation

## Challenges & Solutions
The primary challenge was achieving reliable liquid handling without spills.
This was addressed through careful trajectory planning and the development
of specialized control algorithms that accounted for liquid dynamics. In the end, there was not enough time to finish things.
