Hello, I'm Jason

Hello, I'm Jason! I'm a recent graduate from UMass Amherst, where I got my masters degree in mechanical engineering. I am looking for open positions in virtually any domain. The most important thing to me is that I can work on interesting problems with a great team. Some of my interests are running, meditation, reading, and researching a subfield of machine learning called mechanistic interpretability. You might wonder why a mechanical engineer has so many software projects. The primary reason is that it is cheap and fast to make things that you can see working.
